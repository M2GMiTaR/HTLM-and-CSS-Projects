Kong: Skull Island follows a group of explorers and soldiers who travel to a mysterious, uncharted island, only to discover it's ruled by the massive Kong and filled with deadly creatures. Ghost in the Shell

Ghost in the Shell follows Major Mira Killian, a cyborg with a human brain, who works as an elite operative fighting cybercrime in a futuristic world where technology and humanity are deeply intertwined. As she hunts a dangerous hacker, she begins uncovering hidden truths about her past and the organization that created her. The film blends sleek visuals, intense action, and thought-provoking themes about identity and what it means to be human.

Beauty and the Beast

Beauty and the Beast follows Belle, a bright and independent young woman who becomes the prisoner of a mysterious Beast in his enchanted castle. As she gets to know him, she discovers that beneath his frightening exterior lies a kind and gentle soul, and their growing bond may be the key to breaking a powerful curse. The film blends romance, music, and fantasy in a timeless tale about love, courage, and seeing beyond appearances.
